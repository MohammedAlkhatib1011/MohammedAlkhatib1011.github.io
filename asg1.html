Visit to UTM Digital.

Posted by Mohammed Alkhatib on December 16, 2023

My Reflection

On the 6th of December we had a visit to UTM digital, it was a great and insightful visit the lasted for about and hour. They showed taught me how a support teams works based on how complex is the problem, how important is teamwork and how they work as a team, and when they do brainstorming in the meetings room.

What was more excited for me is the server room seeing it for the first time after years of studying it made me realized all the things i have learnt about it and how it looks like in real life, how networks work and connected to the servers, where the data are stored and how secure are the servers and cold, they showed us all of these things in the visit. The activities benefit us as a student the activities spark excitement/interest on me, after seeing the servers and how the networks work we want to learn more about it.

A picture of the UTM digital staff

The visit to UTM Digital provided a practical perspective on the theoretical knowledge I've accumulated over the years, truly bridging the gap between classroom learning and real-world application. It reinforced my enthusiasm for ICT and has ignited a deeper curiosity to delve into the complexities of networking and data management.
